Runyonii Privacy Policy

About this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Runyonii and how we use it. If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Data Runyonii collects

  1. Your account information, such as your email address, username, last login date, account creation date, profile picture, and your friendlist. We do not store, nor have access to your password, encrypted or unencrypted. The passwords are handled by a third party, a company called Firebase Inc., a subsidiary of Google Inc.
  2. Data that is shared with other users, such as the contents of the messages you send through our service and your friend requests.
  3. Non-user-identifiable information automatically collected by Firebase, such as the number of visitors for different periods and database usage volume.
  4. If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide.

How we use your information

  1. The A class data, that is account information, is used for logging in, sending password reset emails, displaying your profile picture and username for other users, and so on.
  2. The B class data is not used for anything else than delivering messages and notifications.
  3. The C class data is not anything we control and will be collected no matter what. We will only use that data to determine what types of server usage we want to optimize.
  4. The D class data is hard to say anything about since it could contain anything,
None of the information is shared with others exept for with our only third party, Firebase, and in the case of court orders.

Sharing of Data

Runyonii does not sell your personal data. The data is shared only with our only third party, Firebase Inc. Firebase servers and API are used throughout the entire application. None of the information we collect is stored by us. The data is stored only on Firebase's servers. By using our service you consent to their handling of data, described in their Privacy Policy.

Cookies

The Runyonii site does not use cookies. The service still works if you disable cookies through a browser setting.

GDPR Data Protection Rights

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:
  1. The right to access – You have the right to request copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  2. The right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request that we complete the information you believe is incomplete. This is not applicable as we do not publish information.
  3. The right to erasure – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions. This is not applicable for our service because we do not process your data other than in order to provide our service.
  4. The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  5. The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions. This is not applicable for our service because we do not process your data other than in order to provide our service.
  6. The right to data portability – You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

Children's information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity.

Runyonii does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.
